## üéì Understanding the NGM Rate Structure

### **Why $280/hour?**

NGM bills enterprise ML/Data Ops engineers at this rate because:

1. **Base Engineer Salary:** $180,000-220,000/year
2. **Benefits & Taxes:** Additional 30-40% ($54,000-88,000/year)
3. **Overhead Costs:** Office space, equipment, software licenses, training
4. **Corporate Profit Margin:** 20-30% markup
5. **Risk & Liability Insurance:** Professional indemnity, project insurance
6. **Management & Administration:** HR, finance, legal, sales support

**True Cost to NGM per engineer:** ~$350,000-400,000/year  
**Billable hours target:** 1,400-1,600 hours/year (excluding training, admin, downtime)  
**Required billing rate:** $280-300/hour to maintain profitability

### **Corporate Overhead (15%)**

This covers:

- Sales and business development
- Contract management and legal review
- Invoicing and accounts receivable
- Quality assurance and code review processes
- Project management office (PMO) oversight
- Corporate insurance and compliance
- Technology infrastructure and tools
