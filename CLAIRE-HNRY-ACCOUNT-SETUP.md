# Recipe: Setting Up HNRY Account and Adding Team Member

Hi Claire! This recipe will guide you through creating an HNRY account for your coaching business and adding Julian as a team member.

## Prerequisites

- Valid email address (preferably your business email)
- Business information ready
- Government-issued ID or business documentation
- Bank account details for payouts

## Part 1: Create Your HNRY Account

### Step 1: Sign Up for HNRY

1. Go to https://hnry.co.nz (or https://hnry.com.au for Australia)
2. Click **"Get started"** or **"Sign up"**
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Click **"Create account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your email inbox for a verification email from HNRY
2. Click the verification link in the email
3. You'll be redirected back to HNRY

### Step 3: Complete Business Profile

Fill out the required business information:

1. **Business type**: Select "Sole trader" (self-employed individual)
2. **Business details**:
   - Trading name (your business name)
   - Business address
   - Phone number
   - Business description (e.g., "Life and Career Coaching Services")

3. **Personal details**:
   - Full legal name
   - Date of birth
   - Home address
   - IRD number (NZ) or ABN (Australia)

4. **Bank account details**:
   - Account name
   - Account number
   - BSB (if in Australia)

### Step 4: Complete Identity Verification

1. HNRY may request verification documents
2. Upload a government-issued ID (driver's license, passport, etc.)
3. Wait for verification (usually takes a few hours to 1 business day)

### Step 5: Set Up Payment Collection

1. Go to **Settings** → **Payment methods**
2. HNRY handles invoicing and payment collection
3. They will automatically deduct tax obligations and ACC levies
4. Remaining funds are paid directly to your bank account

## Part 2: Add Julian as a Team Member

### Step 6: Access Team Settings

1. Log in to your HNRY Dashboard at https://my.hnry.io
2. Click on your profile or **Settings**
3. Select **"Team"** or **"Access control"**

### Step 7: Invite Julian

1. Click **"Add team member"** or **"Invite user"**
2. Enter Julian's email address: `[INSERT JULIAN'S EMAIL HERE]`
3. Select the appropriate role:
   - **Admin**: Full access (recommended for your developer)
   - **Accountant/Bookkeeper**: Financial access
   - **Read-only**: View-only access

   **Recommendation**: Choose **"Admin"** so Julian can:
   - Access API credentials (if needed)
   - Configure integrations
   - View transactions
   - Help with setup

4. Click **"Send invite"**

### Step 8: Confirm Invitation

1. HNRY will send an invitation email to Julian
2. Julian will need to:
   - Click the invitation link in the email
   - Create an HNRY account (or log in if he already has one)
   - Accept the team invitation
3. Once accepted, Julian will appear in your Team list

## Part 3: Integration Access

**HNRY Integration Details:**

Once Julian is added as a team member, he can:

- Access integration settings for connecting to your booking system
- Set up automated invoicing
- Configure payment notifications
- Handle all technical setup

**No need to send or share credentials** - Julian will get access once he accepts the invitation.

## Part 4: Important Security Settings

### Step 9: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Under **Two-factor authentication**, click **"Enable"**
3. Follow the prompts to set up 2FA using:
   - Authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.) - RECOMMENDED
   - SMS (if available)

### Step 10: Review Security Settings

1. Review **notification preferences**
2. Set up **payment alerts**
3. Check **integration security settings**

## Part 5: Understanding HNRY's Service

### What HNRY Does:

- ✅ Automatically calculates and pays your taxes (income tax, GST)
- ✅ Handles ACC levies
- ✅ Provides invoicing tools
- ✅ Manages payment collection
- ✅ Generates financial reports
- ✅ Provides accounting support

### Service Fee:

HNRY charges 1% + GST of your income (capped annually), which covers:

- Tax calculations and payments
- ACC management
- Accounting support
- Payment processing
- Financial reporting
